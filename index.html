Our Story

Born From Our Own Family's Journey

When our own grandfather needed help, we discovered how hard it was to find the right home. The system felt fragmented — no one seemed to look at his whole story, his medical needs, and what truly made him feel at home.

Our family was able to navigate the gaps because of two decades of hospice care experience. We created Guiding Light Senior Placement so other families don't have to go at it alone — walking with you from the first call to move-in day with patience, honesty, and heart.

"We walk beside families to help them find a home that fits the person — not just the diagnosis."

How It Works

Three Easy Steps

We personalize your search to find a senior living community where your loved one will truly thrive.

  1. 1

    The Initial Discovery Call

    From your very first phone call, Guiding Light Senior Placement listens attentively to understand your unique situation. We do not just look at data; we listen to your story to capture two critical areas:

    • Individual Care Needs: We assess specific required assistance with Daily Living Activities (ADLs) and evaluate specialized care requirements, including memory care transition units.
    • Personal Lifestyle Preferences: We pinpoint what makes a place feel like home — whether that means a small, cozy residential care home or a larger environment with open areas and robust social activities.
  2. 2

    Personalized Vetting & Guided Tours

    We match your care needs and lifestyle preferences against our heavily vetted network of communities, then schedule and accompany you on tours of the top matches to make sure the right questions get asked.

    • Side-by-Side Advocacy: We walk through each environment with you to evaluate the atmosphere, staff engagement, and safety standards.
    • Real-Time Feedback: We help you debrief after each visit to compare how well the community aligns with your social and physical goals.
  3. 3

    Final Placement & Transition

    We guide you through the final selection and paperwork to make move-in day as stress-free as possible.

    • Logistics Coordination: We assist with the community communication and transition details required for a seamless move.
    • Post-Placement Check-in: We follow up after the move to ensure your loved one is adjusting comfortably and receiving exceptional care.
